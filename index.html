Hi! I'm Max Najork - welcome to my homepage! I'm currently a fourth-year student at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, where I am studying Computer Science. This summer, I'm working at Palantir Technologies as a Performance Engineer Intern.

Background

I'm a Bay Area native, born and raised in Palo Alto. True to the Silicon Valley stereotype, I received my first computer when I was seven - a used eMachines eOne running Windows 98, with a modest 433 MHz processor and 64 megabytes of RAM.

I graduated from Palo Alto High School in 2012, and I expect to receive my Bachelors of Science in Engineering from the University of Michigan in May 2016. I've spent the past two summers as an intern at Palantir, and am returning for a third stint this year. Prior to Palantir, I was an intern at the Lockheed Martin Solar and Astrophysics Lab in Palo Alto, where my responsibilities included administrating an internal requirements tracking tool for the Geostationary Lightning Mapper project.

I have experience with C++ and Java, and have worked with Xcode and Eclipse. I'm an avid learner, and enjoy pursuing personal projects to explore new technologies.

I hold dual citizenships with the United States and Germany.
